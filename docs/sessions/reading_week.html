Reading Week

Changes before 22/23
  • Add Learning Outcomes for each week
  • Highlight relevance of readings to Assessment 1 and overall Learning Outcomes.

Overview

Important

This week’s learning objectives are:

  1. Get caught up on any rmissed eading(s).
  2. Start to think about the research question(s) for your Policy Briefing.
  3. Start to think about how you might ask this question(s) of the data.

You should be doing the readings that will support your answers to Assessment #2. And, looking ahead to the Final Assessment and where you might find ideas or literature to support your thinking, I’d suggest having a browse of the Full Bibliography. This is a working document and I will add more items as and when I come across them or new works are published!

Readings
